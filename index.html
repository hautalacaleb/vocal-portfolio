About Me

üé∂ My Musical Journey

How I discovered my passion for singing and vocal work!

My musical journey is an interesting one because it actually started relatively recently. My whole life I never thought I was a creative person and focused most of my attention on learning academic skills or job specific skills. I bounced around between many different fields; from the military, to container unloading, to a dental lab making dentures and many different personal projects. I never stayed in one field very long and was never able to find my passion. Until about just under 2 years ago I fell in love with singing. It brought so much freedom to just fall into the emotions of a song and belt your heart out. Since then it has been almost my sole focus and I have put everything I have into improving. Taking the technical and academic style of learning skills I have forged in my life and combining them with the endless creativity of singing and vocal work to build up my skillset and get to where I am today.

This is a never ending journey of course. Every day I am tackling new challenges and learning new or improving current vocal techniques, allowing me to breathe new life and emotion into the songs I sing or characters I portray.
